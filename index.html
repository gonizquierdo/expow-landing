A bit about Expow

Business relations between projects and influent people are growing up year after year.

Brands, startups and projects pay tons of money to content creators and influencers to reach more people through standard, old school advertising deals, with a lot of obstacles such as fraud, underpayments, false profiles, lack of metrics.

Our vision is to make these relations as smooth as possible, enabling every content creator or media channel to expose their advertising space to the whole market, automating marketing interactions between all parties with state of the art blockchain technology.

Value of community and utilities

Building community is important for democratazing exposure and increasing value of advertising spaces. Content creators through their audiences can provide brands and statups a higher ROI than traditional ADS or Macro influencers.

Growing the expowsers community will help reach a more fair and just ads market. With more transparency and revenue.
